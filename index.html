Welcome

This website shares real lived experiences of the care system. It exists to raise awareness, give care-experienced people a voice, and help others better understand the realities of care.

About the Project

The care system affects thousands of people, yet many voices go unheard. This project was created to share honest experiences, encourage understanding, and support those who have lived in or alongside the care system.

Mark Loat created this website to highlight lived experience and promote dignity, respect, and meaningful change.
